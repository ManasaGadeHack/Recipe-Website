Chicken Fry

This Spicy Chicken Fry is a delightful and aromatic dish, ideal as a side or a starter. It features tender chicken pieces marinated in a rich blend of spices, including ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and a homemade spice mix. The chicken is then cooked until golden brown and crispy, ensuring each bite is packed with flavor. Enhanced by sautéed onions and fragrant curry leaves, this dish is sure to please spice lovers and anyone looking for a hearty meal. Serve it hot with steamed rice, roti, or enjoy it as a standalone appetizer for an unforgettable culinary experience.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the chicken with ginger-garlic paste, salt, turmeric powder, and lemon juice.

step 2

Mix well to ensure the chicken is evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes for maximum flavor.

step 3

In a heavy-bottomed pan, dry roast the cardamoms, cloves, cinnamon, cumin, fennel, black peppercorns, and coriander seeds on low heat until they are aromatic.

step 4

Add the dried red chilies and roast until they become crisp. Allow the roasted spices to cool, then grind them into a fine powder. Set this spice blend aside.

step 5

In the same heavy-bottomed pan, heat 4 teaspoons of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Stir in the curry leaves and fry for another minute.

step 6

Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until the chicken changes color from pink to white. Mix in the prepared spice blend, ensuring the chicken is well coated. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

step 7

If the chicken appears dry, add about ½ cup of water, cover again, and cook on low heat for another 10 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked. Finally, add the slit green chilies and fresh coriander leaves, cooking on high heat until the moisture evaporates and the chicken is crispy. Once cooked, remove from heat and serve hot, garnished with additional coriander leaves. This dish pairs wonderfully with steamed rice, roti, or can be enjoyed as a standalone appetizer.

ingredients

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1 kg bone-in chicken

½ teaspoon turmeric powder

Salt to taste

2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste

2 green cardamoms

4 cloves

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon fennel seeds

1 teaspoon black peppercorns

2 tablespoons coriander seeds

6 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)

4 teaspoons oil (vegetable or any cooking oil)

4 onions, finely chopped

2 green chilies (slit, adjust to taste)

A handful of curry leaves

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

tools

Pots

Strainer

Measuring Spoons

Skillet
